About Bralio

Bralio is a corporate English coach for Latin American professionals who already speak English but freeze up in meetings, hesitate in interviews, or feel their career is capped by how they sound. We deliver short daily lessons through WhatsApp and provide on-demand coaching whenever you need to prep for a call, review a message, or practice out loud.

We built Bralio for the gap between "I can speak English" and "I can lead in English." Most professionals never close that gap. The skill is there. What is missing is English practice designed for the high-stakes corporate moments that actually decide careers: the meeting, the presentation, the interview, the follow-up.

Why we built this

One-on-one tutors cost $40 to $80 an hour and require scheduling. Apps built for beginners do not help a mid-career product manager or engineer who already gets by in English and wants to sound sharper in their next meeting. Bralio lives in the messaging app you already use, costs a fraction of a human tutor, and focuses entirely on professional communication: the meetings, the presentations, the interviews, the follow-ups, the moments where how you sound decides whether you are heard.

Who's behind it

Bralio is built by Francisco and Maricruz.

Francisco is an engineer based in the United States. Maricruz is a corporate English coach who works directly with Latin American professionals and runs a community of learners focused on confidence and delivery in English-speaking workplaces. Every feature of Bralio comes from real patterns she sees every day: the hesitation, the filler words, the meetings where brilliant people hold back because English slows them down. Promotions get decided in those rooms.

Together they are building the coach they wish more of their friends and colleagues had. The moments that matter in a career happen in English: the meeting where you need to speak up, the interview where you need to sound sharp, the feedback call where you need to lead. Bralio is built for those moments. The goal is simple: help Latin American professionals be fearless in corporate English.
